Privacy Policy

At AUTODRIVE, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide during registration or contact forms.
  • Transaction Data: Information related to vehicle listings, chats, and interactions between buyers and sellers.
  • Usage Data: Data about how you use our website, such as pages visited, time spent, and IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers through our chat system.
  • Improve our platform's functionality and user experience.
  • Send you updates, promotions, or notifications (you can opt out at any time).
  • Ensure the security of our platform and prevent fraudulent activities.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With your consent, such as sharing contact details with a seller during a transaction.
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.
  • With service providers who assist us in operating our platform (e.g., hosting services), under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption for chat communications and secure storage for personal information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting our support team.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Request information about how your data is being used.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, such as remembering your preferences and analyzing site usage. You can manage cookie settings in your browser.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you via email if significant changes are made.

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact our support team.
